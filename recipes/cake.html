Cake

To lighten up your mood, cake is what you make

People mostly eat cake to celebrate something. People consider it as a sweet delicacy.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cup all purpose flour
  • 4 egg
  • 2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 11/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk

Recipe Instructions:

  • Cream together butter-sugar and then blend with beaten eggs
  • Combine flour mix and beaten eggs
  • Bake the cake as per your convenience
  • Check with a knife or skewer if it is cooked and serve.
