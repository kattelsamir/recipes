Tea

The true beauty you wanna see, have a sip of tea.

A drink very popular among many people. Many people choose to drink tea just after waking up.

Ingredients:

  • Clean water (1 glass)
  • Sugar (2 tablespoon)
  • Tea leaves

Recipe Instructions:

  • Take 1 cup of water and heat it in a low flame.
  • Add 2 table spoons of sugar.
  • Increase the intensity of the flame.
  • Add some tea leaves.
  • Allow it to boil for some time.
  • Pour the tea in a glass and serve it well.
